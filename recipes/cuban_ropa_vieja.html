Cuban Rope Vieja

This rope vieja is great served on tortillas or over rice. Add sour cream, cheese, and fresh cilantro on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 pounds beef flank steak
  • 1 cup beef broth

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet ver medium-high heat. Add flank steak and cook until browned, about 4 minutes per side
  2. Transfer steak to a slow cooker; pour in beef broth and tomato souce.
  3. Cover and cook on the Low for up to 10 hours, or on High for 4 hours.
  4. Shred steak in the slow cooker with two forks.
